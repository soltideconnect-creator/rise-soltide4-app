Q: I don't see the "Unlock for Testing" button. What do I do?

A: Make sure you:
   1. Are using an Android device (not iPhone)
   2. Are on the "Stats" tab
   3. Have scrolled down to "Upgrade to Premium"
   4. Have the latest version of the app
   
   If still not visible, contact soltidewellness@gmail.com

Q: Do I need to pay for premium during testing?

A: No! The "Unlock for Testing" button gives you free access
   to all premium features during the closed testing phase.

Q: Will my data be saved?

A: Yes! All your habits, streaks, and sleep data are saved
   locally on your device. They persist even if you close
   the app or restart your phone.

Q: Can I test on multiple devices?

A: Yes, but each device will have separate data. The app
   stores data locally, not in the cloud (for now).

Q: What happens after testing ends?

A: When the app launches publicly, you'll need to purchase
   premium via Google Play ($4.99 one-time). Your test data
   will remain on your device.

Q: I found a bug. How urgent is it?

A: Report all bugs! We'll prioritize:
   - CRITICAL: App crashes, data loss
   - HIGH: Features don't work
   - MEDIUM: UI issues, minor bugs
   - LOW: Suggestions, improvements
