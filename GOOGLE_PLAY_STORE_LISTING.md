## FAQ for Store Listing

**Q: Is Streak really free?**
A: Yes! Streak is free to download and use with all features included. There's an optional one-time purchase to remove ads and support development.

**Q: Does Streak require an internet connection?**
A: No! Streak works 100% offline. All your data is stored locally on your device.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No account needed! Just download and start tracking your habits immediately.

**Q: Can I track multiple habits?**
A: Yes! Track unlimited habits with custom emojis, colors, and reminder times.

**Q: What happens to my data?**
A: Your data stays on your device. You can export it anytime as a backup file.

**Q: Does Streak have dark mode?**
A: Yes! Streak has perfect dark mode support that follows your system preference or can be toggled manually.

**Q: Can I customize reminder times?**
A: Yes! Each habit can have its own custom reminder time.

**Q: What are streak milestones?**
A: When you reach 7, 30, or 100 consecutive days, you'll see confetti celebrations and special achievements!

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Absolutely! Streak works offline, requires no account, and never sends your data anywhere.

**Q: Can I use Streak on multiple devices?**
A: Currently, data is stored locally. You can export from one device and import to another.
